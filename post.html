Is the Universe Really What We See?

Human eyes electromagnetic spectrum ke sirf ek limited part ko hi dekh paati hain. Iska matlab ye hai ki universe ka majority hissa directly invisible hai. Dark matter aur dark energy jaise concepts isi baat ko support karte hain ki jo kuch universe ko control karta hai, wo hamesha visible nahi hota.

Dark Matter and Dark Energy: The Invisible Reality

Scientific observations ke according, galaxies ki motion aur cosmic expansion ko sirf visible matter se explain nahi kiya ja sakta. Dark matter gravity provide karta hai, jabki dark energy universe ke expansion ko accelerate karti hai. Ye dono milkar universe ke future ko define karte hain.

Space-Time Is Not Linear

Einstein ki theory of relativity ke baad ye clear ho chuka hai ki space aur time alag-alag entities nahi hain. Black holes aur gravitational waves jaise phenomena prove karte hain ki time har jagah ek jaise speed se flow nahi karta. Universe dynamic hai, static nahi.

Cosmic Cycles and the Future of Earth

Earth ka future sirf climate change ya technology se decide nahi hoga, balki cosmic factors bhi ek important role play karte hain. Solar activity, asteroid threats aur galactic cycles hamesha se planetary evolution ka hissa rahe hain.
