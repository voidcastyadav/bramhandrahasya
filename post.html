The Hidden Structure of the Universe

Universe hamesha se human curiosity ka sabse bada source raha hai. Lekin jitna hum space ke baare me jaante hain, usse kaafi zyada cheezein aisi hain jo abhi bhi unseen aur unexplained hain. What Do We Actually See?

Jab hum raat ke aasman ko dekhte hain, to hume stars, planets aur galaxies nazar aati hain. Lekin scientific studies ke mutabik, ye visible universe total reality ka sirf ek chhota sa hissa hai. Jo hum dekh paate hain, wo universe ka majority part nahi hai.

Dark Matter: The Invisible Framework

Dark matter directly light emit ya reflect nahi karta, isliye ye invisible hai. Lekin galaxies ki rotation aur gravitational behavior ko samajhne ke liye dark matter ka existence zaroori ho jaata hai. Ye universe ka structural backbone maana jaata hai.

Dark Energy and Cosmic Expansion

Observations batate hain ki universe sirf expand nahi kar raha, balki uski expansion speed time ke saath increase ho rahi hai. Dark energy is acceleration ka sabse strong explanation hai. Ye energy space ke fabric ke saath interact karti hai.

Is Space-Time Fixed or Flexible?

Einstein ki theory of relativity ke baad ye clear ho gaya ki space aur time alag entities nahi hain. Massive objects space-time ko bend kar dete hain, jiska effect gravity ke form me hum observe karte hain. Isliye universe static nahi, balki dynamic hai.
